What is "iamthedd"?

iamthedd is essentially a story generator that depicts a night out with friends. You are the designated driver, and while some of your friends might not have remembered everything from last night, you do. This this is your [disgruntled] recollection of the events that took place. All you have to do is add your friends to a crew, give each crew member a personality, select a venue, and submit to generate a unique story about your night out.
