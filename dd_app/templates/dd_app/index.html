What is "iamthedd"?

Iamthedd is essentially a story generator that places you and your crew in a night out for some drinks and you are the designated driver, reporting on the events that took place. Think of it as the classic "Mid-Libs" game with a twist. All you have to do is add your friends to a crew, set up a brief personality profile, define some event variables(venue, holiday, birthday, ect. ), and submit you crew to generate a unique story about you night out.
